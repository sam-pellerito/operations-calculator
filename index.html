How the math works

Formula (one-time event): 1) Marketing budget from savings B = S * r 2) Additional revenue ΔR = B * mROI 3) New revenue R1 = R0 + ΔR 4) Incremental contribution ΔContribution = ΔR * cm 5) New margin % ≈ (R0*M0 + ΔContribution) / R1
